Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli,the former captain of Indian cricket team,is one of the most popular cricketers of the world. He is one of the best batsman and fielder of all time. He is the best Indian batsman of this decade with the highest number of runs in all formats of the game and a remarkable strike rate. He was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India.He is popularly called as King Kohli by his fans for his extrordinary talent and remarkable achievements. Kohli is regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world and has numerous records to his name. He made his debut for the Indian cricket team in 2008 and has since become a regular member of the team across all formats.
Kohli has also captained the Indian cricket team across all formats and has led the team to many notable victories, including a historic Test series win in Australia in 2021. In terms of his individual achievements, Kohli has scored over 12,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs) and over 7,000 runs in Test cricket, making him one of the most prolific run-scorers in the history of the game. He has also been awarded numerous awards, including the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year in 2017 and 2018. Off the field, Kohli is known for his fitness and disciplined lifestyle, and is also involved in various philanthropic activities through his foundation, the Virat Kohli Foundation.
